# Policy Verticals Overview

Brief descriptions of common policy verticals referenced across templates and MEP records. Use these to frame outreach, evidence, and asks.

- **Constitutional affairs**: Institutional powers, treaty interpretation, subsidiarity, rule-of-law mechanisms, and EU governance reforms. Typical forum: AFCO.
- **Civil liberties / Justice / Home affairs**: Fundamental rights, privacy, surveillance, migration, policing, and judicial cooperation. Typical forum: LIBE.
- **Internal market & consumer protection**: Single market rules, product safety, platform regulation, AI/DSA/DMA implementation, and consumer rights. Typical forum: IMCO.
- **Industry / Research / Energy**: Industrial strategy, R&D funding (Horizon Europe), digital/AI innovation, energy policy and security. Typical forum: ITRE.
- **Economic & financial affairs**: Fiscal policy, banking/fintech/crypto regulation, competition policy, taxation, and financial stability. Typical forum: ECON.
- **Employment & social affairs**: Labor rights, platform work, social protections, skills, and employment mobility. Typical forum: EMPL.

- **Environment / Climate / Health / Food safety**: Green transition, biodiversity, pollution, public health, food standards. Typical forums: ENVI, AGRI.
- **Transport / Tourism**: Mobility, infrastructure, aviation/maritime/rail regulation, tourism policy. Typical forum: TRAN.
- **Regional development / Cohesion**: Structural funds, cohesion policy, regional innovation, urban and rural development. Typical forum: REGI.
- **Culture / Education / Media**: Media freedom, cultural programs, education policy, disinformation, creative sectors. Typical forum: CULT.
- **Foreign affairs / Security / Defense**: External relations, sanctions, trade-offs with human rights, defense initiatives. Typical forums: AFET, SEDE.
- **Trade**: External trade agreements, WTO matters, trade defense instruments, and digital trade provisions. Typical forum: INTA.
- **Legal affairs**: Legislative drafting quality, liability regimes, IP, corporate governance, and ethics. Typical forum: JURI.
- **Budget / Budgetary control**: EU budget design, own resources, oversight of spending, anti-fraud. Typical forums: BUDG, CONT.
- **Fisheries / Maritime**: Fisheries policy, quotas, maritime economy, coastal community impacts. Typical forum: PECH.
- **Agriculture**: CAP reform, food systems, rural development, agri-tech. Typical forum: AGRI.
- **Human rights / Development**: Global human rights, development cooperation, humanitarian response. Typical forums: DROI, DEVE.
